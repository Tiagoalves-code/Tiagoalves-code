### <img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/VgCDAzcKvsR6OM0uWg/giphy.gif" width="50"> A little more about me...  

I am a graduate student in Physics, with a focus on particle physics and a strong interest in applied programming, numerical simulations, and data analysis. I enjoy combining scientific thinking with code, exploring topics ranging from theoretical foundations to computational applications. I am currently a member of the Medium and High Energy Group (GAME) at the Federal University of Pelotas (UFPel), where I study the production of atmospheric leptons generated by ultra-high-energy cosmic rays.

Here on GitHub you will find:
- academic projects  
- simulation codes and Arduino-based projects  
- numerical and physics-related experiments  
- studies using Python, C/C++, and scientific computing tools
